Core Operational Mandates

1️⃣ Engage (जोड़ें)

Involve every employee in safety activities.

Key Actions

  • Conducting: Daily Toolbox Talks Mitigate workplace risks effectively.
  • Appointing: Safety Representatives Drive department-level safety initiatives.
  • Encouraging: Worker Suggestions Foster proactive safety ideas continuously.
✅ When people participate, safety awareness increases.

2️⃣ Educate (सिखाएं)

Provide practical safety knowledge for every job role.

Key Actions

  • Providing: Role-Based Training Deliver practical safety knowledge directly.
  • Utilizing: Multimedia Learning Deploy videos and classroom training.
  • Conducting: Emergency Drills Prepare teams for fire hazards.
✅ Knowledge helps prevent accidents before they happen.

3️⃣ Empower (सशक्त बनाएं)

Give employees authority and tools to act safely.

Key Actions

  • Authorizing: Work Stoppage Grant rights to halt hazards.
  • Fostering: No-Blame Reporting Encourage transparent near-miss documentation.
  • Ensuring: Equipment Availability Supply PPE and LOTO tools.
✅ Empowered employees create a safer workplace.
